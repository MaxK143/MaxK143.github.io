Kerbal Space Program

I am a math and engineering minded person, and this game is a dream for someone like that. It takes your engineering designs and brings a variety of some easier and genuinely difficult logistical challenges to them.


Elden Ring

In my opinion the best game ever made. The story cowritten by George R Martin was simply amazing and awe-inspiring. The gameplay was incredibly challenging as all fromsoft games are regarded as, but Elden Ring was the most rewarding experience because of this difficulty. It is the game I am most proud of completing 100% of.


Terraria

I have played terraria for most of my life as a gamer. I’ve beaten the regular game several times, and beat the calamity mod four times, once being on the hardest difficulty. The developers have made many updates to the game over its long life making it a better experience every time crafting it into what it is today, a very smooth, fun, and exciting experience.


Ready or Not

This game terrifies me and I love it. I don't like horror games, but this game isn’t terrifying in a normal sense. You get immersed in the game, and your mind races as every door you breach could be your death if you make a single mistake. The accuracy and realism of the guns, enemies, and everything makes the immersion so smooth.


Cookie Clicker
This is the original braindead afk game, and I love it. Whether I was watching youtube, eating food, or even working sometimes, I would be running this game. It is a simple concept with a fun well executed theme, and I enjoyed the 1,500 hours I have in it.
